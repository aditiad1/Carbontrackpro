Troubleshooting

  • The calculator doesn't load: Make sure to replace "YOUR_APP_URL" with your actual Replit app URL.
  • The iframe is blocked: Some websites have Content Security Policies that block iframes. You might need to adjust these settings.
  • The calculator looks too small/large: Adjust the padding-bottom percentage in the container div to change the aspect ratio.
  • You need a different theme: Change "theme=light" to "theme=dark" in the iframe URL.
